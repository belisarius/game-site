Changes

I've worked at Aksys Games as a localization editor for about six years now. I got this job more or less right out of collegeâ€”after a short stint as a contract film editor. It's been fun, and I've done a lot of work that I'm proud of. I'll also really miss the people I've worked with, who've become some of my best friends. I'm not very good at talking about my feelings in an intelligent, human way, but my co-workers were easily the best part of my job. The second best was getting the oportunity to help bring over things that people enjoyed. Working at Aksys gave me the chance to be a part of something that made a lot people happy (not all the time, but, you know, enough) which is an oportunity I was very, very lucky to have.

The plan, at least, is to keep working in video games: Localization is what I've got experience in, so I'll likely be doing that for a while, but hopefully before too long I'll be making my own games. Maybe Gakuen Necro and Lumberjaction and all the others will finally see the light of day! Maybe if I'm especially fortunately, I'll get another chance to make stuff that you enjoy.

In any event, I've wanted to start up a blog to talk about games for a long time, and I figured since I'm turning my life upside down now is the perfect time to give myself another thing to do.
